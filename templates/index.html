Voice Commands

English

  • "ALPHA" — wakes ALPHA up
  • "Take me to [place]" — routes to a destination
  • "Take me from [A] to [B]" — routes between two places
  • "Stop navigation" — cancels the active trip
  • "How far is the destination" — remaining distance

Français (French)

  • "ALPHA" — réveille ALPHA
  • "Emmène-moi à [lieu]" — itinéraire vers une destination
  • "Arrête la navigation" — annule le trajet en cours
  • "À quelle distance" — distance restante
